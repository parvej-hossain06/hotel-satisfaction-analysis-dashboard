🏨 Hotel Satisfaction & Value Analysis Dashboard

 Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes hotel customer reviews to evaluate customer satisfaction, value-for-money perception, traveler behavior, and hotel performance.

The dashboard provides actionable insights for hotel management teams to identify service gaps, improve customer experience, optimize pricing strategies, and enhance overall guest satisfaction.

---

 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer satisfaction across different traveler segments.
- Evaluate value-for-money (VfM) perception among customers.
- Identify hotels with high dissatisfaction rates.
- Compare satisfaction trends across countries and demographics.
- Support data-driven decision-making for service improvement initiatives.

---

Dashboard Pages

Hotel Performance Analysis:

Provides a detailed overview of hotel-level performance and customer sentiment.

Key Insights:
- Business travelers report the lowest value-for-money ratings.
- Family travelers demonstrate the highest satisfaction levels.
- Several hotels show dissatisfaction rates above 70%.
- VfM perception remains below overall satisfaction scores.

💡 Business Recommendations

Improve Value Perception
Focus on pricing strategies and service enhancements for business travelers who report the lowest VfM scores.

Address Dissatisfaction Drivers
Investigate hotels with high dissatisfaction rates and implement targeted improvement plans.

Enhance Family Traveler Experience
Leverage strengths in family travel segments through loyalty programs and family-focused services.

Monitor Customer Sentiment
Track satisfaction trends regularly to identify emerging issues and opportunities.
